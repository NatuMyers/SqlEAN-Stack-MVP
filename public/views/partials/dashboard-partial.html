About Me

I'm a fourth year Computer Engineering and Economics student at Queen's University ready to enter the world of technology. My desire to go into tech is something that hasn't changed in almost a decade.

I've designed and developed all sorts of projects: mobile, desktop and full stack web applications, both personal and professional. I've also started two organizations from the ground up: Queen's Conference on Business and Technology, and PromoBands. Learn more about these in the Portfolio section.

Last summer, I developed software as an intern for the Mobile and Online Banking Team at RBC, Canada's largest bank. Before that, I was a Software Intern at AG-NAV Inc., and a Teaching Assistant for the data structures course (ELEC 278) at Queen's.

When I'm not studying, coding, or working, I like to play music (guitar and piano), soccer, and a little squash.
